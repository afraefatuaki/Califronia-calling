Surrounded by smoke and inspired by the communities’
resilience, our design team reimagined the California
Grizzly and used it to create custom tees, pins and
posters.

Our t-shirts (kids and adult sizes), pins, and posters will be
available for purchase at our exclusive pop-up shop during
our SF Design Week Studio Crawl this Friday, June 21. You
can choose what amount to donate. All proceeds will go to
the American Red Cross to help the communities that are
still displaced.
